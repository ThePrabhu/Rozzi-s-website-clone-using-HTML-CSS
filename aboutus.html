About Us

Born in Melbourne, Rozzis is the collaborative outcome of seasoned food & business operators who have combined their past 30+ years’ industry experience to create a truly exciting offer. Vibrant energy, eclectic style, and a the perfect mix of tradition and innovation is in our blood - qualities that align perfectly with our passion for pizza. We believe in keeping it simple yet extraordinary: fresh ingredients, hand-stretched dough, and authentic flavours inspired by best pizzerias from across the world.

Rozzis' commitment to quality and convenience is unmatched. We offer a variety of delicious slices ready to grab and go, without compromising on flavour or freshness. Whether you’re in the mood for a classic Margherita, a hearty Pepperoni, or something more adventurous, we’ve got something to satisfy your cravings.
